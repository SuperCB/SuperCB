Having been hunted to  extinction in the wild  by the early 20th century, the European bison was reintroduced to the wild in various European countries by the 2010s, following  captive breeding  programmes. It is the heaviest wild  land animal  in Europe, and individuals in the past may have been even larger than their modern-day descendants.
