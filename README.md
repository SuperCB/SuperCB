His enslaved personal servant,  William "Billy" Lee , is on horseback behind him. Trumbull served on Washington's staff as an  aide-de-camp  early in the  American Revolutionary War  and painted this portrait from memory about five years later while studying in  London . The painting has been in the collection of the  Metropolitan Museum of Art  in New York City since 1924.
