>Pinnularia  is a genus of freshwater  algae  in the class Bacillariophyceae, the  diatoms . An elongated elliptical  unicellular organism , it is usually found in ponds and moist soil. The  cell walls  are composed chiefly of  pectic  substances on a rigid  silica  framework, and are composed of two halves known as  thecae  (or, less formally, valves).
